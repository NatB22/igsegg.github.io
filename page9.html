Reviewer database

We're looking to help early career researchers (ECRs) gain experience in reviewing journal articles by collating a database of ECRs in the cryospheric sciences interested in reviewing scientific articles. This information will be shared with editors of journals with glaciological and cryospheric content to help them find reviewers more easily, and boost visibility of ECRs. To keep the database up-to-date we will renew entries and data once a year.
This form is aimed at early career researchers but open to any scientists. This reviewer database is organized by the International Glaciological Society (IGS) Early-career Glacier Group (EGG), which was established in 2019. For questions please contact egg@igsoc.org!
